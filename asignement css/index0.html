Welcome to North Vancouver's 5 pin bowling center

Open since 1961, this family oriented establishment has been owned and operated by 3 time Canadian Bowling Champion Richard Grubb since 1983.


We have 16 lanes to host leagues, glowbowling, bumper bowling, ball ramps, a party room which also glows, video games and snacks.

We participate in fund raisers, parties for Christmas, the office and team parties. Fun for everyone. Give us a call or drop in.
